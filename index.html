According to me personally "LoL" is the greatest word in history.
It stands for "laughing out loud". No, it does not mean "Lots of Love" as some people might assume.
Lol used to be used as an acronym to emphasise great amusement in chat conversations.
Nowdays there are lots of different ways to LOL.
For example, "LMAO" is great to use when you find something to be extreamly funny.
It stands for "Laughing my a** off"
Or did anyone say someting awkward that you don't know what to reply to?
Then just say lol!
It's a great coverup for when you simply can't get yourself to laugh.
Lol is a great filler word in general.
You have nothing to contribute to a vaugly amusing conversation?
LoL!
My personal favorite is the "ROFLMAO" verison. It stands for
"rolling on the floor laughing my a** off"

Consider this a "PSA" that everyone should LoL more.
Lols makes the world a better place :')
